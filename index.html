Benefits you can gain

Save expenses
Use plastic bottles and coffee cans to make containers and plants combined. You can even hang them on the wall with wires.
Improved air quality
Plants can filter out nitrogen dioxide. Vertical gardens are even better than trees at sucking up pollution from daily life.
Grow your own food
Eating your own herbs and vegetables is an incredibly rewarding experience.Having plants around the house make the environment more soothing.
Sense of comfort
Connect with nature has profoundly positive effects on human well-being. And can increase staff job satisfaction and productivity.
Noise reduction effectively
The leaves and stems of plant can scatter high-frequency sound waves. Vertical garden can be a good barrier against nuisance noise.
Warm and cool building
In summer vertical garden can suck heat from the surrounding air via evapotranspiration, cooling the inside of the building.Meanwhile block the wind and slows heat loss in winter.
